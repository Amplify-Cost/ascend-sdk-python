Customer Pilot Strategy

Ready for Pilots

Platform is production-ready for limited customer pilots with these capabilities:

  • Full workflow approval automation
  • NIST/MITRE/CVSS compliance tracking
  • Real-time policy enforcement
  • SLA monitoring and escalation
  • Immutable audit logging

Pilot Limitations

  • Average approval time <2 hours
  • SLA compliance >95%
  • Policy false positive rate <5%
  • API uptime >99%
  • Customer satisfaction score >8/10
